Some people have 'to be read' lists of hundreds of books long. I imagined what such a person's house might look like. What if the books weren’t just on the furniture, but were the furniture?

This illustration is a little love letter to that idea: a cozy corner built entirely out of books, where a reader lives inside the world her library has made for her.
