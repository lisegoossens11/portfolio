Hi, I’m Lise.
An illustrator based in Ghent, Belgium.

I love creating illustrations that are playful yet thoughtful. My work can make complex or sensitive subjects feel more approachable, but I’m just as happy drawing the small moments of daily life.

I work on editorial projects and beyond, and enjoy exploring how illustration can bring the right emotion, mood and meaning to a project.
