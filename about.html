Hi there! I'm Lise, an illustrator based in Ghent, Belgium. In 2020 I received my master's degree in illustration at LUCA school of arts. Ever since then I've been doing illustration projects. I like to draw from my imagination, and I'm inspired by the books I read, the clothes I wear, and everything I see and overhear in my daily life.

For illustration work or private commissions, please send an email to hello@lisegoossens.com.
